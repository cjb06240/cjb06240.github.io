This show is full of funny remarks and sometimes inappropriate jokes. It keeps onself engaged and interested, even when things start to repeat themselves. It has enough surprises in each season to keep interesting, and continues to surpass the expectations placed on the show in regards to entertainment. So I would recommend this series if you're into these type of shows.
