Schmidt

Schmidt is as big as a drama queen as they come. He is definilty more of a self centered character, who usually is all about the ladies. Until one day he met Cece, Jess' friend, who he fell madly in love with. Throughout the whole series he constatnly sought after his love, until finally they did get married. Their relationship was a roller coaster which brought an interesting flavor to the show.
Schmidt beleives he is the "top dog" our of all the roommates. He is a clean freak who has troubling history with his view of his body image. He brings a personality that clashes against his roommates, but at the same time works well as the characters begin to gain chemistry and developemnt throughout the season.
