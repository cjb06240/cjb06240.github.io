Jess

Jess is the show's "Main character", she is a lovable, carefree, emotional female lead that has proven time and time again to be the star of the show. She's bubbly, caring and hilarious. She works as a school teacher for most of the series, which suits the type of character she is.
Jess is always trying to have fun, whehter it's hanging out with her roommates, or having disastrously funny realtionships. Throughout the series she has many relationships, but constantly has an on and off fling with her roommate Nick. Along with her boyfriends, Jess only has one friend outside of her roommates, and that is the model CeCe. Jess typically keeps to herself and spends most of her time with those close to her.
